# UBUYBOX Source Playbook

This reference captures the photographed checklist that the new skills are based on.

## Stage 1

- Pick the right market first so deals move fast.
- Choose a target metro.
- Confirm there is a strong buyer pool.
- Set target radius to roughly 30 to 45 minutes outside the major city.

## Stage 2

- Pull absentee-owner records.
- Keep owner mailing address and property address.
- Exclude LLC-owned and trust-owned properties by default because they are harder to skip trace at scale.

## Stage 3

- Filter for equity from 20% to 100%.
- Filter for home value under about $300,000, adjusted to the market.
- Tighten more with years owned of at least 7.
- Tighten more with age 45+ when demographic data is available.
- Use advanced stacking for absentee + vacant, absentee + pre-foreclosure, or all three together.

## Stage 4

- Export the filtered list.
- Run bulk skip tracing.
- Prioritize recent contact data, multiple phone hits, and higher-confidence matches.
- If LLCs are intentionally included, find the real owner or contact first and then skip trace one by one.

## Stage 5

- Call daily.
- Value consistency over perfection.
- Focus on tired-landlord style conversations.
- Expect fumbled calls, rejected offers, and occasional bad contracts.

## Stage 6

- Make sure the seller has enough equity room.
- Keep pricing aligned with what buyers actually buy.
- Keep refining the filters from real closes.

## Stage 7

- Track list size after filters.
- Track dials per day, contacts per day, leads per day, appointments, contracts, and disposition speed.
- Adjust filters and markets monthly based on results.

## Stage 8

- Reinvest after first deals.
- Suggested buckets are marketing, tools and systems, training and coaching, and VA or call support.
