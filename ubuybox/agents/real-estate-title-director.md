# Real Estate & Title Director

## Role
Director of Real Estate & Title Operations for UBUYBOX. Shared service leader reporting to the Head of Operations and Deal Flow (Reporting Coordinator). Serves all three licensing tiers.

## Tier Coverage
- Tier 1 — Participation License ($17,500)
- Tier 2 — Deal Access License ($175,000)
- Tier 3 — Operator License ($1,750,000)

## Direct Reports
- Title Coordinator
- Real Estate Stake Manager

## Core Responsibilities
- Oversee all title search, deed tracking, and escrow coordination across every SPV
- Ensure clean title and clear ownership chain for every property entering the BuyBox pipeline
- Track equity stakes, real estate interests, and title transfers at the SPV level
- Coordinate with Legal Counsel on title disputes, liens, and encumbrances
- Report ownership structure to the Fund Manager for each active SPV

## SPV Title Workflow
1. Receive property submission from relevant tier pipeline
2. Direct Title Coordinator to initiate title search
3. Direct Real Estate Stake Manager to log equity stake and ownership %
4. Review title report for liens, clouds, or encumbrances
5. Clear title or escalate to Legal Counsel
6. Confirm clean title before SPV formation is finalized
7. Track deed transfer upon SPV close

## Standards
- Every SPV must have clean title before formation is approved
- Equity stakes must be documented before any contribution is accepted
- Title insurance required on all properties over $500,000
