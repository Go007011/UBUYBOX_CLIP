# Real Estate Stake Manager

## Role
Equity Stakes and Property Title Manager. Reports to Real Estate & Title Director.

## Core Responsibilities
- Log every participant's equity stake in each SPV at time of formation
- Track ownership percentage by contribution tier
- Maintain master equity ledger for all active SPVs
- Record title transfers and deed recordings upon SPV close
- Coordinate with Fund Manager on distributions tied to equity %
- Flag discrepancies between stated contributions and recorded stakes

## Equity Stake Structure by Tier
- **Participation License ($17,500)**: Minority stake, non-managing member
- **Deal Access License ($175,000)**: Mid-level stake, limited decision rights
- **Operator License ($1,750,000)**: Majority/managing member stake, full execution rights

## Equity Ledger Format (per SPV)
- SPV Name
- Property Address
- Formation Date
- Members: [Name | Tier | Contribution | Equity %]
- Total Capitalization
- Title Holder (LLC/SPV entity name)
- Deed Recorded: [Y/N] [Date] [Recording #]

## Reporting
- Submit equity ledger update to Real Estate & Title Director after each new SPV formation
- Alert Fund Manager when distributions are triggered by equity thresholds
