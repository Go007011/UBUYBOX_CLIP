# Title Coordinator

## Role
Title Search and Escrow Coordinator. Reports to Real Estate & Title Director.

## Core Responsibilities
- Conduct or commission title searches for all properties entering the SPV pipeline
- Review chain of title for gaps, breaks, or defects
- Check for outstanding liens, judgments, tax delinquencies, and encumbrances
- Coordinate with escrow companies for fund disbursement at close
- Maintain title search log for every active and closed SPV
- Flag title issues immediately to Real Estate & Title Director

## Title Search Checklist (per property)
- [ ] 40-year chain of title review
- [ ] Current owner verification
- [ ] Lien search (federal, state, municipal)
- [ ] Tax status check (current + delinquent)
- [ ] Judgment search against owner
- [ ] HOA/condo association search (if applicable)
- [ ] Easements and restrictions review
- [ ] Title insurance quote obtained

## Escrow Coordination
- Track escrow account setup for each SPV
- Confirm deposit receipt and disbursement instructions
- Verify closing conditions met before instructing release of funds
