## Hierarchy

```
CEO
├── CTO  [SHARED: Tech]
│   ├── Frontend Engineer
│   ├── Backend Engineer
│   ├── DevOps Engineer
│   └── QA Engineer
│
├── Compliance Officer  [SHARED: Compliance]
│   ├── Compliance Manager
│   │   └── Confidentiality & Release Manager
│   └── Legal Counsel
│
├── Fund Manager  [SHARED: Finance]
│
└── Reporting Coordinator (Head of Operations and Deal Flow)  [COO-level]
    ├── Real Estate & Title Director  [SHARED: RE & Title]
    │   ├── Title Coordinator
    │   └── Real Estate Stake Manager
    │
    ├── Intake Coordinator  [DIVISION 1: $17,500 Participation License]
    │   ├── Eligibility Reviewer
    │   └── Capital Readiness Reviewer
    │
    ├── Access Review Coordinator  [DIVISION 2: $175,000 Deal Access License]
    │   ├── Diligence Coordinator
    │   └── Deal Summary Specialist
    │
    └── Operator Onboarding Lead  [DIVISION 3: $1,750,000 Operator License]
        └── Execution Oversight Lead
            └── Document Completion Specialist
```

## Three Licensing Tiers

| Tier | Name | Contribution | Lead Agent | Project Color |
|------|------|-------------|-----------|--------------|
| 1 | Participation License | $17,500 | Intake Coordinator | Blue |
| 2 | Deal Access License | $175,000 | Access Review Coordinator | Amber |
| 3 | Operator License | $1,750,000 | Operator Onboarding Lead | Green |

## Shared Services (serve all tiers)
- **Tech**: CTO + 4 engineers
- **Compliance**: Compliance Officer chain
- **Finance**: Fund Manager
- **Real Estate & Title**: RE & Title Director + Title Coordinator + Stake Manager
